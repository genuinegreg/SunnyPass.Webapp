SunnyPass

Keep your password to yourself

SunnyPass allow you to keep your password and private notes safe while accessing them easily on any of your devices.

Store your password and private note in a "safe" identified by your unique safe's secret and locked by a password. Your safe's secret can be used to create a secure peer to peer network in order to access the content of your safe on multiple devices without it's content ever stored elsewhere than your own devices.



Or

To create your safe you need to generate a random safe's secret and a pick a strong password. You will need both to access your safe content.
